## Project Overview
This repository contains my submission for the CSE0001 Digital Literacy Project.
The project focuses on understanding digital literacy, building a professional online presence, learning responsible digital communication, and gaining awareness about cybersecurity and cybercrime prevention.

## Tasks Included
Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
Created an infographic explaining the importance of digital literacy, digital tools used by students, safe internet practices, and basic email etiquette.

Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
Created and updated professional profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle to build a digital portfolio and maintain a professional online presence.

Task 3 – Coding Platform and Google Form
Explored an online coding platform to practice programming skills and created a Google Form to collect responses and understand how digital tools can be used for data collection.

Task 4 – Email Etiquette
Analyzed a situation of poor digital communication and explained how proper email etiquette and professional communication can improve online interactions.

Task 5 – Cybercrime Case Study
Studied a cybercrime case and created a prevention checklist to spread awareness about safe online practices and methods to avoid cyber threats.
